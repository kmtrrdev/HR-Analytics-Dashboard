# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI.
The goal is to analyze employee data and uncover meaningful insights related to workforce distribution, attrition, salary trends, and employee satisfaction.

This dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze workforce demographics
* Identify patterns of employee attrition
* Understand salary distribution across roles
* Evaluate employee satisfaction and performance
* Explore relationships between different HR factors

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 👥 Workforce Demographics

* Majority of employees fall within the **20–29 age group (~60%)**
* Most employees hold a **Bachelor's degree**
* The **Technology department** dominates with the highest number of employees

💡 Insight:
The company relies heavily on young professionals, which may increase turnover risk.

---

### 📉 Attrition Analysis

* Higher attrition among employees with **low tenure (0–2 years)**
* Employees living closer (0–20 distance) still show noticeable attrition

💡 Insight:
Attrition is more related to early career dissatisfaction than commuting distance.

---

### 💰 Salary Insights

* Most employees are concentrated in **lower salary ranges (below 100K)**
* Very few employees earn above **300K**

💡 Insight:
Salary distribution is skewed, which may impact retention of top talent.

---

### 😊 Job Satisfaction

* Balanced distribution between satisfied and dissatisfied employees
* Many employees fall in **neutral or moderately satisfied categories**

💡 Insight:
Employees are not highly disengaged, but there is room for improving satisfaction.

---

### ✈️ Business Travel

* Around **70% of employees travel occasionally**
* Few employees travel frequently

💡 Insight:
Business travel does not appear to be a major factor affecting employee dissatisfaction.

---

### ⚖️ Gender Distribution

* Male and Female employees are relatively balanced
* Similar satisfaction levels across genders

💡 Insight:
No strong gender-based disparity in satisfaction or attrition.

---

### 🎓 Education Field

* Majority of employees come from:

  * Computer Science
  * Information Systems

💡 Insight:
Workforce is highly tech-oriented, aligning with department distribution.
